Sponsors

Why Sponsors?

  • Help expand on more teaching spaces and resources to communities that are underserved
  • Open the doors to a potential career path for kids
  • Create a lasting impact on the next generation of kids
  • Support inclusivity for individuals with mental disabilities who want to learn how to code
  • Promote and grow your organization / business / school and connect with new customers

What Your Donation Will Go Towards

  • Expanding our teaching resources beyond our local communities established in Davis and Woodland
  • Awarding prizes and rewards for our end of the year event for children, volunteers, teachers, and parents
  • Providing safe transportation for our volunteers
  • Enhancing teaching resources provided during sessions such as worksheets, starter kits, etc.
  • Supporting us in keeping our website online for our users
